Instructor Notes

Training guide — running a cohort

1 · Before the cohort

  • Student applies free on RBLP, requests you as instructor, and pays for exam prep on your ATP store (or COOL/CA funds it).
  • Send the student their module password for RBLP's exam prep training page.
  • They review each module for their level and answer the reflection questions about their leader/follower experience — arriving with real examples.

2 · What you cover — the five competency domains

  • Team Climate (all levels) — shared perceptions, attitudes, and emotions; a positive climate lifts morale, creativity, problem-solving, and resilience.
  • Team Cohesion (all levels) — the bond that keeps a team united through adversity; social + task cohesion.
  • Individual Purpose (all levels) — the desire and determination to build a meaningful future through work.
  • Team Learning (Coach & Trainer) — experiencing, reflecting, deciding, and acting to improve and innovate.
  • Organizational Learning (Trainer) — how organizations gain and keep a competitive advantage through improvement at scale.

3 · Session length

  • RBLP — Modules 1–3 · ~3 instructional hours
  • RBLP-C — Modules 1–4 · ~4 hours (+ lunch & breaks)
  • RBLP-T — Modules 1–5 · ~5 hours (+ lunch & breaks)

4 · Running the session (oral-exam rehearsal)

  • Facilitate a live discussion of each domain — this is rehearsal for the oral exam.
  • Have every student speak to their own leader/follower examples; coach them to articulate clearly.
  • Rehearse the oral-exam format so they arrive confident.

5 · After the session

  • Student schedules the oral exam on RBLP's scheduling page with the voucher code, uploads your training completion certificate, and takes the exam.
  • Move their status along here: applied → paid → scheduled → passed.
