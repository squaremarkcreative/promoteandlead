What is RBLP

Certified leaders who build resilient teams.

The Resilience-Building Leader Program (RBLP®) is the only nationally recognized series of leadership certifications that showcase real-world knowledge, skills, and experience. RBLP-certified leaders know how to build and lead resilient teams that can overcome adversity, adapt to change, and grow stronger over time. Learn More. Lead Better.®

Positive climate

Leaders create the conditions of safety, trust, and respect where teams do their best work.

Team cohesion

Cohesive teams stay committed to each other and to the mission when the pressure is on.

Shared purpose

A clear sense of purpose gives people the resilience to persist through adversity and change.

The RBLP Framework

Five competency domains

The RBLP Framework organizes leadership into five competency domains. Each certification builds on the last. RBLP assesses the first three, RBLP‑C adds Team Learning, and RBLP‑T adds Organizational Learning.

01

Team Climate All levels

The shared perceptions about attitudes and emotions that define a team. A positive climate enhances morale, creativity, problem-solving, and resilience.

02

Team Cohesion All levels

The bond that keeps a team united and committed, even in the face of adversity, built from both social cohesion and task cohesion.

03

Individual Purpose All levels

The desire and determination that drives people to create a meaningful and fulfilling future, often through their work.

04

Team Learning Coach & Trainer

A continuous cycle of experiencing, reflecting, deciding, and acting together to improve, innovate, and solve problems.

05

Organizational Learning Trainer

How organizations gain and maintain a competitive advantage through continuous improvement and innovation at scale.

How to use COOL/CA in 4 steps

Apply free on the RBLP site

The RBLP application is free and takes under 10 minutes. When asked, request Promote and Lead Solutions, LLC as your exam prep instructor.

Request COOL/CA funding for prep and exam

Create a learning goal in your branch portal (Army IgnitED / Air Force COOL), which requires two quotes: one for exam prep training and one for the certification exam. After you apply, RBLP provides both quotes for you.

Get funded & complete your prep

COOL/CA funds your exam prep training about a week before your course date. You complete your instructor-led, online prep with us during your scheduled cohort.

Redeem your voucher and schedule your exam

Once your funding is approved, RBLP sends you a voucher code. Schedule your oral exam on RBLP's exam scheduling page: select your certification level, complete your admin details, choose a date and time, and upload your training certificate. Add the exam to your cart and pay with your voucher code, then pass your exam and earn your certification.
